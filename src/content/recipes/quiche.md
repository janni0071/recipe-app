## Steps

1. Cut the leeks and mushrooms in fine slices. Chop the onion finely. Cook all of them on low heat with a bit of olive oil/butter for about 15min. Season with salt and pepper. Cook the lardons in a pan if you do not choose to put in ham.
2. In a big bowl, mix the eggs, the cream, the milk, and the grated cheese. Season the mix.
3. Pre-heat the oven at 200°C. Butter a tart mould or place baking paper in the mould, then place the dough inside evenly. Pick the Bottom of the dough with a fork to let the air flow.
4. Evenly spread the veggies and ham onto the dough. Pour the creamy mix on top evenly, careful not to go over the border of the dough.
5. Bake in oven for 40 minutes and check with a knife to see if the batter mix is cooked enough. If it is too liquid and/or the top is not golden, leave to bake some more time.
